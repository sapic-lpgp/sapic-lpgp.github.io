The images below illustrate the spoke structures that emerge in magnetron discharges, characterised by a localised enhancement of the ionisation rate $S_i$.

## Spoke dynamics

Spokes rotate azimuthally at a fraction of the $\mathbf{E} \times \mathbf{B}$ drift velocity. Their angular velocity $\Omega$ satisfies:

$$
\Omega = \frac{v_\theta}{R} \approx \frac{1}{R} \frac{E_r}{B_z}
$$

where $R$ is the target radius, $E_r$ the radial electric field, and $B_z$ the axial magnetic field.
