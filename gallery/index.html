Spoke dynamics

Spokes rotate azimuthally at a fraction of the E×B\mathbf{E} \times \mathbf{B}E×B drift velocity. Their angular velocity Ω\OmegaΩ satisfies:

Ω=vθR≈1RErBz\Omega = \frac{v_\theta}{R} \approx \frac{1}{R} \frac{E_r}{B_z}Ω=Rvθ​​≈R1​Bz​Er​​
