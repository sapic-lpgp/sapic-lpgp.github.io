SAPIC is a 3D-PIC AMR code developed at the Laboratoire des Gaz et des Plasmas (LPGP) in Paris, France. It is designed for the simulation of magnetized plasmas in complex geometries, with a particular focus on magnetron applications. SAPIC is build as an adaptive mesh refinement (AMR) code with a particle-in-cell (PIC) method for plasma simulation, and is developed in C++ with a Python interface for pre- and post-processing.

The SAPIC team

Adrien and Max are the main developers of SAPIC. We are both postdoctoral researchers at the Laboratoire des Gaz et des Plasmas (LPGP) in Paris, France, and have a background in plasma physics and computational methods.
